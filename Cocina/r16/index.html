Cocina 3D — Vista previa

PR #16: Ventanas de verdad y luz bonita

Branch: alzados-dimensiones

## Summary

Major viewer and model improvements across three areas: realistic windows, lighting system overhaul, and architectural refinements.

### Windows
- Realistic window structure: white metal border plate (2mm), 5cm panel frames with 5mm gaps, rubber gaskets, and glass panes
- Glass rendered as single inward-facing planes (invisible from outside)
- Persiana boxes: white plastic, no back face (flush on wall)

### Lighting
- 5-mode light cycle: Ambient - Dawn - Day - Dusk - Night
- Wireframe-while-moving for smooth interaction (requestAnimationFrame at 60fps)
- Warmer LED color, reduced focus intensity
- Night mode: dark blue windows, ambient so nothing is pitch black
- Dawn and Ambient modes: warm golden / pastel blue windows

### Faucet and sink
- Cylinder-based gooseneck faucet with horizontal handle
- Undermount sink (no rim), open-top basin
- Countertop split around sink cutout, no edge lines on counter

### Architecture
- Furniture and vuelo raised to 220cm (10cm gap to ceiling)
- Nicho de techo: 10cm tall (was 20cm)
- Door height: 203cm (Spanish standard)
- Door depth: 8cm, only 2cm protrusion into room
- West wall switch: centered on wall, facing into room

### Other
- createBoxGeometrySkip supports multiple face skips via string
- Base cabinet top faces removed (hidden under countertop)
- Cornisa Norte click toggles focus lights
- PR preview page shows title and description
